MANTRA PUJA - PLAY STORE UPLOAD GUIDE

Follow these steps to upload and publish the Version 5.0.0 update to the Google Play Store:

---

STEP 1: Log in and Select the Application
1. Log in to the Google Play Console (https://play.google.com/console).
2. Select "Mantra Puja" from your app list.

---

STEP 2: Create a Production Release
1. In the left navigation menu, scroll down to the "Release" section.
2. Click on "Production" (or "Testing" if publishing to an internal/closed track first).
3. Click the "Create new release" button at the top right of the page.

---

STEP 3: Upload the App Bundle
1. In the "App bundles" drag-and-drop zone, upload the signed AAB file:
   File Path: Play Store/Test 5/app-release.aab
2. Wait for the upload to complete and for Google to process the bundle.
3. Verify that the console displays:
   - Version Code: 5
   - Version Name: 5.0.0
   - Package Name: com.mantrapuja.official

---

STEP 4: Configure Release Details
1. Google Play Console will automatically populate the Release Name as "5.0.0 (5)" or similar.
2. Under "Release Notes", copy and paste the contents of:
   File Path: Play Store/Test 5/release-notes.txt
   (Include support for both English and translated locales as configured).

---

STEP 5: Check and Update Store Listing Assets (If Needed)
If you need to update graphics, navigate to "Grow" > "Store presence" > "Main store listing":
1. App Icon: Upload the 512x512 PNG found in assets.
2. Feature Graphic: Upload the 1024x500 PNG found in assets.
3. Screenshots: Upload screenshots (screenshot-1.png to screenshot-7.png) to Phone and Tablet tabs.
4. Support URLs: Verify URLs in "App content" / "Store settings":
   - Privacy Policy: https://www.mantrapuja.com/privacy-policy
   - Terms: https://www.mantrapuja.com/terms-and-conditions
   - Refund Policy: https://www.mantrapuja.com/refund-policy

---

STEP 6: Review and Rollout
1. Click "Save" at the bottom right.
2. Click "Review release" at the bottom right.
3. Confirm there are no blocking errors (Warnings about de-obfuscation files can be safely ignored or resolved by uploading native debug symbols).
4. Click "Start rollout to Production" (or click "Start rollout to Testing" if on a pre-release track).

---

Verification Checklist:
[ ] Signed with existing keystore 'my-release-key.keystore'
[ ] Alias matches 'my-key-alias'
[ ] Version Name is '5.0.0' and Version Code is '5'
[ ] Package Name is exactly 'com.mantrapuja.official'
[ ] Debug signatures are NOT present in the final binary
